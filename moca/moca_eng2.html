1st trial, say:

"This is a memory test. I am going to read a list of words that you will have to remember now and later on. Listen carefully. When I am through, tell me as many words as you can remember. It doesn’t matter in what order you say them.”

Then:

Read the list of words once. Check off each word recalled

When patient has finished(has recalled all words or can recall no more words) say:

"I am going to read the same list a second time. When I am through, tell me as many words as you can remember, including words you said the first time.”

Then:

Read the list and then tick checkbox as per 1st trial

Finally say:

“Try to remember these words as I will ask you to recall these again at the end of the test."
