Adrenaline administration

use Adrenaline 1:1000 ampule 0.5ml by deep intramuscular injection, if necessary this can be repeated at 5-15mins intervals with a max of 3 doses. All vital signs should be recorded every 5-10 mins and documented all symptoms and treatment given. Standing order

Dosage for 1:1000 formulation is 0.01ml/kg with up to a max of 0.5ml.
