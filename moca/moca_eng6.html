6. Abstraction

Practice

“To which category do an orange and a banana belong to?” If the patient answers in a concrete manner, ask once: “Can you tell me another category these items belong to?” If the subject does not respond correctly [Fruits], the examiner says: “Yes, and they both belong to the category Fruits”. No additional instruction or clarification is given.

Tell me which category do a train and a boat belong to?

OK: means of transportation, travelling, vehicles.

Not OK: made of iron, have engines, consume oil, petrol or gasoline.

Correct Wrong
Train & Boat

Tell me which category do North and South belong to?

OK: Cardinal directions, cardinal points, directions, hemispheres, regions.

Correct Wrong
North & South

Tell me which category do a Drum and a Flute belong to?

OK: Musical instruments

Not OK: Made from wood or any other material, produce sound.

Correct Wrong
Drum & Flute
