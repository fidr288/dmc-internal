Have your child complete these four questions | 請讓您的孩子完成以下四條問題


The Childhood Asthma Control Test is a SIMPLE TEST suitable for asthma sufferers (aged 4 to 11 years).¹ The test contains SEVEN QUESTIONS that you can complete online. It will provide you with a snapshot of how well your child’s asthma has been controlled over the last four weeks.


  • 1: Let your child respond to the first four questions (1 to 4). If your child needs help in reading or understanding the question, you may help, but let your child select the response.
    • Complete the remaining three questions (5 to 7) on your own and without letting your child's responses influence your answers. There are no right or wrong answers.
  • 2: Click an answer button for each question. You will see a number score populate the column to the right.
  • 3: When all seven questions have been answered, press the "TOTAL SCORE" button to view your score.
  • 4: Take a print-out of the test results to the doctor or nurse to talk about your child's total score.

It is a good idea to complete the test at regular intervals as asthma symptoms can vary from month to month and at different times of the year.
If you would like to take the test now, please click BEGIN TEST. It should take you around 1 minute to complete.
